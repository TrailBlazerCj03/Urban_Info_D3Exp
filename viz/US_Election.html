US Election

Do people still support Trump in the US midterm selection? We classify the attitudes into three types and let each type be a specific value.
Trump supporters—-1
Netural—-0
Trump opposers—(-1)

For example, expressions like “Trump still president” are considered to be 1, “President Trump is helping Beijing win in the South China Sea” considered to be -1, ‘Tracking the president's visits to Trump properties’ are considered to be 0.
To make the results readable, I use D3Plus sto visualize the results.
